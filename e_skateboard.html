Overview

I started the project by mounting the pulley to the wheel. I had to drill straight holes through the wheels to mount the pulley. It wasn't easy, because I didn't have a drill press and had to drill straight holes using a hand drill. I didn't drill the holes perfectly, so I had to widen those. To prevent bolts coming through the wheel I used one big washer. I drilled holes in it, so I could put bolts trough it.

Installing the strain gauges

To install the strain gauges, I made the top and the bottom of the trucks flat. That gives enough room to install strain gauges on flat surface. Then I glued strain gauges to the trucks and soldered wires to them.

Mounting the motor
